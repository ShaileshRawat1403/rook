# Origin

Rook began as an experimental fork of Goose.

The goal is not to maintain a Goose distribution. The goal is to explore a different direction for local AI agents: a lighter Rook-native desktop, a guided CLI/TUI experience, and a workflow surface that makes agent actions visible, inspectable, and easier to control.

Rook keeps the useful open-source foundations where appropriate, but the product identity, documentation, packaging, and user experience are being rebuilt around Rook.
